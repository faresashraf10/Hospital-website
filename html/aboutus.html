Welcome To Lifeline
Hospital


Lifeline Hospital established in 2015 by Dr. Ahmed. With a prime location in the heart of Alexandria City, we are proud to be known as one of the most reputable and largest hospitals in the entire governorate.

At our 70 bed hospital, we are committed to providing high-quality health care services to our patients. Our team of
experienced and dedicated medical professionals is equipped with the knowledge and expertise to deliver exceptional care across a wide range of medical specialties.

Lifeline Hospital is known for emergency and acute care services and has earned a very good reputation over the last years.
Our medical team consists of highly experienced doctors over 60 specialists, consultants, and university professors at near to 50 medical specialties and subspecialties distributed in its integrated medical departments.
Our entire team has large experience in the healthcare sector, in delivering exceptional patient care and providing high-quality medical and surgical treatments.
Our experience in medical and tourism fields, combined with our multilingual staff management system, allows us to communicate effectively with patients from various nationalities and meet their needs easier and smoother.

In 2023, we embarked on a comprehensive hospital upgrading and renovation process. This transformation has allowed us to significantly increase our operating capacity,
more than tripling our previous capacity. We have also invested in state-of-the-art medical equipment to ensure that our patients receive the best possible care.
